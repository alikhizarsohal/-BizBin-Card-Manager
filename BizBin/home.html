About Us

**BizBin** is a mobile application designed to help you organize and manage your business cards. We understand the importance of keeping track of your professional contacts, and BizBin makes it easy to do so. With BizBin, you can:

  • Scan and store business card information directly from your phone.
  • Organize your contacts with customizable tags and categories.
  • Search for specific contacts quickly and easily.
  • Share your own digital business card with others.
